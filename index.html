Our Services

What house cleaning services do we provide?

Our cleaning services offer two options: one-time spring cleaning or a recurring weekly (or fortnightly) cleaning service. We are dedicated to meeting all of your domestic cleaning requirements, which include ironing, dusting, polishing, and vacuuming. Our goal is to provide you with a customized and convenient cleaning experience that is tailored to your home.

One-off home cleaning service

This is a specialized one-off spring cleaning service designed to provide an intense and thorough cleaning of your home. Depending on the size and condition of your home, the cleaning visit can take up to 6 hours to ensure every aspect is meticulously addressed.

With just one payment, you can enjoy a fully professional clean that will leave your home spotless while you're at work or going about your daily activities. Our dedicated team will work diligently to ensure every corner of your home is sparkling clean, giving you peace of mind and a fresh living space.

The cost of a one-off clean is dependent on the location of your home. To obtain the precise prices for your area, kindly fill out our enquiry form, and our team will provide you with the accurate pricing information.

Our clients in various areas of the capital commonly book this service for a duration of 4-6 hours. This time frame allows our cleaning professionals to deliver a comprehensive and satisfactory cleaning experience for your home.

We will match you with a local domestic cleaner and schedule the spring clean based on your availability. Additionally, our service includes comprehensive public liability insurance that covers the duration of the cleaning visit, ensuring your peace of mind and protection.
